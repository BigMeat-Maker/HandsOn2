Welcome to the Savannah

The savannah is a vast grassland ecosystem characterized by its unique wildlife and resilient plant species. Explore the beauty and significance of this incredible habitat.

Lion

The lion, often referred to as the "king of the jungle," is a majestic predator of the savannah.

Rhino

Rhinoceroses are large herbivores known for their impressive horns and thick skin.

Elephant

The African elephant is the largest land animal and a vital part of the savannah ecosystem.

Baobab Tree

The baobab tree is known as the "tree of life" for its water storage and nutritious fruit.

Elephant Grass

This tall grass provides shelter and food for many savannah animals.

Manketti Tree

The manketti tree produces nutritious nuts that are a vital food source for wildlife.
