Welcome to the Rainforest

The rainforest is a dense, lush ecosystem home to a wide variety of unique animals and plants. Discover the wonders of this vibrant habitat.

Mountain Gorilla

The mountain gorilla is a powerful primate that inhabits the dense forests of Central Africa.

Jaguar

The jaguar is a stealthy predator, known for its strength and ability to swim through rainforest rivers.

Capybara

The capybara is the world's largest rodent, often found near water in the rainforest.

Orchids

Orchids are vibrant and diverse flowers that thrive in the humid rainforest environment.

Bromeliads

Bromeliads are unique plants that grow on trees and collect water in their central cups.

Epiphytes

Epiphytes grow on other plants and trees, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.
