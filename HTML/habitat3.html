Welcome to the Deep Ocean

The deep ocean is a mysterious and extreme environment, home to some of the most unique creatures and plant life on Earth.

Deep Sea Angler Fish

The deep sea angler fish is known for its bioluminescent lure, which attracts prey in the pitch-black depths of the ocean.

Barrel Eye Fish

The barrel eye fish has a transparent head, allowing it to see through the darkness of the deep ocean.

Deep Sea Catshark

The deep sea catshark is a small, bottom-dwelling predator that thrives in extreme ocean depths.

Giant Green Anemone

This large sea anemone is found in deep waters, where it thrives on ocean currents for food.

Phytoplankton

These microscopic ocean plants form the base of the marine food chain and produce much of Earth's oxygen.

Red Algae Seaweeds

Red algae seaweeds are deep-water plants that help sustain oceanic life through oxygen production.
